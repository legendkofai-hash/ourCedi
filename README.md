OurCedi

OurCedi is a web-based platform designed to educate the world about the history of the Ghanaian cedi and how it has endured through time and inflation.

Concept & Structure

 The platform consists of multiple pages, each tailored to specific content areas:
 Home Page – Introduction and overview of the platform.
 Services Page – Details of offerings and features.
 Newsletter Page – Updates and subscription options.
 Cedi Page – Historical insights and educational content about the cedi.
 Contact Page – Communication details and inquiry form.
 Sign Up Page – User registration and account creation.
 About Us Page – Background information about the project and its mission.

 Features

 Each page contains content relevant to its title.
 Multimedia elements such as images, videos, and hyperlinks enrich the learning experience.
 Accessibility attributes are included to support screen readers.
 Contact details are provided for direct communication.

 References to external sources (e.g., Wikipedia, Google Search) are included to provide 
 additional context and credibility.
